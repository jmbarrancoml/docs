## Create your account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email signup">
    1. Visit [freiya.app/auth/signup](https://freiya.app/auth/signup)

    1. Visit [freiya.app/auth/signup](https://freiya.app/auth/signup)
    2. Click **Continue with Google**
    3. Select your Google account
    4. Grant permissions to Freiya
    5. You're in! No email verification needed
  </Tab>

## Add your first paper

Let's add a research paper to your library. Freiya supports three methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search by DOI">
    The fastest way to add papers with complete metadata.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Add Paper">
        Find the **Add Paper** button in the top-right corner
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select DOI search">
        Choose **Search by DOI** from the options
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enter the DOI">
        Paste the DOI (e.g., `10.1234/example.doi`)
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review and save">
        Freiya automatically fetches title, authors, abstract, and more. Click **Save**
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://placehold.co/800x500/4F46E5/white?text=Add+Paper+Modal" alt="Add Paper modal with DOI search tab and input field" />
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
    Find DOIs on journal websites, Google Scholar, or at the bottom of PDF papers.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manual entry">
    Add papers that don't have a DOI or when you want to customize metadata.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Add Paper">
        Open the Add Paper dialog
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select manual entry">
        Choose **Manual Entry** tab
      </Step>

      <Step title="Fill in details">
        Enter title, authors, year, publication, and abstract
      </Step>

      <Step title="Save the paper">
        Click **Save** to add to your library
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Upload PDF">
    Upload a PDF and Freiya extracts metadata automatically.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Add Paper">
        Open the Add Paper dialog
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select PDF upload">
        Choose **Upload PDF** tab
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select your file">
        Browse and select a PDF from your device
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review and save">
        Verify the extracted metadata and click **Save**
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
    PDF uploads are limited by file size based on your tier (10MB free, 50MB Pro).
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Start an AI chat

Now let's try Freiya's AI chat feature:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to chat">
    Click **Chat** in the sidebar or press `Ctrl/Cmd + N` for a new chat
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a model">
    Choose an AI model from the dropdown (Free tier users see basic models)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a question">
    Type your question in the input box. Try asking about your paper!

    **Example:** "Analyze the impact of remote work on employee productivity and well-being"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get AI response">
    Watch as the AI streams its response in real-time
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/chat-interface.png" alt="Chat interface showing AI conversation with streaming response" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
Your chats are automatically saved and titled. Find them in the sidebar under "Chats".
</Tip>

## Try the PDF viewer

If your paper has a PDF, you can read and annotate it in Focus mode:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a paper">
    Go to your paper library and click on a paper
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click View PDF">
    Click the **View PDF** or **Focus** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Highlight text">
    Select any text to see the highlighting toolbar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a color">
    Pick a highlight color (yellow, green, blue, red, purple, orange)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a comment">
    Optionally add a comment to your highlight
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/focus-highlights.png" alt="PDF viewer with highlighted text and annotation toolbar" />
</Frame>

All your highlights are saved and can be exported for your writing later.

## Create a collection

Keep your research organized by creating collections:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Collections">
    Click **Collections** in the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click New Collection">
    Click the **New Collection** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your collection">
    Enter a name like "Thesis Chapter 1" or "Machine Learning Papers"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add papers">
    Drag papers into your collection or use the "Add to Collection" button
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Free tier users can create 2 collections. Upgrade to Pro for 20 collections.
</Note>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many papers can I add on the free tier?">
    The Free tier includes 5 papers total with a limit of 5 papers per month. Pro tier gives you 500 papers and 500/month. Unlimited tier has no limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upload my own PDFs?">
    Yes! You can upload PDFs on all tiers. File size limits are 10MB (Free), 50MB (Pro), and 100MB (Nova/Unlimited).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do AI usage limits work?">
    Each model type has a monthly message allocation based on your tier. Usage resets on the 1st of each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my papers and citations?">
    Yes! You can export citations in multiple formats including BibTeX, RIS, APA, MLA, Chicago, and more. Highlights can be exported as Markdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my research data private?">
    Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, secure, and private. Only you can access your papers, chats, and annotations. See our privacy policy for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
