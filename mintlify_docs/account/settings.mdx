## Account settings

Manage your core account information and security.

### Profile information

Update your personal details:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click your profile icon ‚Üí **Settings**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Account tab">
    Navigate to the **Account** section
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit information">
    Update your name, email, or profile picture
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to apply updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

++IMAGE: **Settings page Account tab showing editable fields for name, email, profile picture, and a Save button**++

**Editable fields:**
- Display name
- Email address (requires verification if changed)
- Profile picture (coming soon)
- Bio/description (coming soon)

Changing your email requires verifying the new address before it takes effect.
</Note>

### Change password

Update your password for enhanced security:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account settings">
    Navigate to **Settings ‚Üí Account**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Password section">
    Scroll to **Change Password**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter passwords">
    - Current password
    - New password
    - Confirm new password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save new password">
    Click **Update Password**
  </Step>
</Steps>

++IMAGE: **Change Password section showing three password fields (current, new, confirm) with show/hide icons and an Update button**++

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of letters and numbers recommended
- Special characters recommended

<Tip>
Use a unique password you don't use elsewhere, and consider a password manager.
</Tip>

## Display preferences

### Dark mode

Toggle between light and dark themes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Dark Mode setting">
    **Settings ‚Üí Appearance ‚Üí Dark Mode**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose mode">
    - **System** - Matches your OS setting (default)
    - **Light** - Always use light theme
    - **Dark** - Always use dark theme
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply instantly">
    Theme changes immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

++IMAGE: **Appearance settings showing three radio buttons for System, Light, and Dark mode with icons representing each option**++

**Quick toggle:**
You can also toggle dark mode anytime with:
- Floating button (bottom-right corner)
- Keyboard shortcut: `Ctrl/Cmd + D`
- Top menu ‚Üí Theme icon

<Tip>
Dark mode reduces eye strain during late-night research sessions and saves battery on OLED screens.
</Tip>

### Cancel subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Subscription">
    **Settings ‚Üí Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Find **Cancel Subscription** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose timing">
    - Cancel immediately (rare)
    - Cancel at period end (recommended)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm cancellation
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens:**
- Access continues until period end
- Auto-downgrade to Free tier
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can resubscribe anytime

<Note>
No refunds for partial billing periods, but you keep access until your paid period ends.
</Note>

## Notification settings

Configure how Freiya notifies you about events.

### In-app notifications

Control toast notifications that appear in the app:

**Notification types:**
- Success messages (paper added, chat saved)
- Error alerts (limit reached, operation failed)
- Information (feature tips, updates)
- Warnings (approaching limits, important changes)

**Settings:**
- Enable/disable all notifications
- Set duration (3s, 5s, 10s)
- Position (top-right, bottom-center, etc.)
- Sound on/off

++IMAGE: **Notification Settings showing toggles for different notification types and dropdown for duration and position preferences**++

### Usage alerts

Get notified when approaching limits:

- **80% capacity** - Warning when nearing limits
- **100% capacity** - Alert when limit reached
- **Monthly reset** - Reminder when limits reset
- **Boost low** - Alert when boost credits are low

Enable or disable each alert type individually.

## Privacy settings

Control your data and privacy.

### Data export

Download all your Freiya data:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Privacy">
    **Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Data**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Export Data">
    Request data export
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for processing">
    Export prepares (usually < 5 minutes)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download">
    Download ZIP file with all your data
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Export includes:**
- All papers and metadata
- All chats and conversations
- Annotations and highlights
- Collections
- Account information

++IMAGE: **Privacy section with "Export My Data" button and explanation text about GDPR data portability rights**++

### Account deletion

Permanently delete your account and all data:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Privacy settings">
    **Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Data**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find Delete Account">
    Scroll to danger zone
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Delete Account">
    Red **Delete My Account** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm with email">
    Type your email to confirm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Final confirmation">
    Acknowledge data loss and confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Danger>
Account deletion is PERMANENT! All papers, chats, annotations, and data will be deleted within 30 days. Export your data first if you want to keep anything.
</Danger>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Settings changes not saving">
    Try these steps:
    1. Check internet connection
    2. Refresh the page
    3. Clear browser cache
    4. Try a different browser
    5. Contact support if persistent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't change email">
    Verify:
    - New email isn't already in use
    - Verification email received
    - Clicking verification link
    - Old email still accessible
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Dark mode not working">
    Check:
    - Browser supports dark mode CSS
    - No conflicting browser extensions
    - Try setting to "Dark" instead of "System"
    - Clear cache and reload
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Payment method won't update">
    This is handled by Stripe:
    - Verify card details are correct
    - Check card isn't expired
    - Sufficient funds available
    - Contact your bank if declined
    - Try a different card
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
