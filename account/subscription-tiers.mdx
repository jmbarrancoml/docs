## Free tier

<Note>
The Free tier is perfect for students, casual users, or anyone wanting to try Freiya before committing.
</Note>

### What's included

**Paper management:**
- 5 papers total in your library
- 5 papers can be added per month
- Full metadata fetching via DOI
- Tags and notes on all papers
- Citation generation

**Collections:**
- 2 paper collections
- 2 chat collections
- Full organization features

**AI chat:**
- Access to basic AI models
- Standard monthly message limits
- Full chat history and search
- Share conversations

**PDF viewer:**
- Full Focus mode access
- Basic highlighting (all colors)
- Comments on highlights
- Export annotations

**Other features:**
- Institution search
- Dark mode
- Multi-language (EN/ES)
- Email support

### Best for

- Students starting their research journey
- Trying Freiya for the first time
- Managing a small focused project
- Side projects with minimal papers

### Limitations

- **Paper limit** - 5 papers may fill up quickly
- **Collections** - Only 2 collections limits organization
- **Basic models** - No access to advanced AI models
- **Monthly cap** - 5 papers/month prevents bulk imports

<Tip>
Start with Free and upgrade when you need more capacity. Your data transfers seamlessly!
</Tip>

## Managing your subscription

### Upgrade your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings ‚Üí Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose new tier">
    Click **Upgrade** on your desired tier
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Enter payment details via Stripe checkout
  </Step>

  <Step title="Instant activation">
    New limits apply immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downgrade your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings ‚Üí Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select lower tier">
    Click **Change Plan** and choose a lower tier
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm downgrade">
    Review what changes and confirm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Effective date">
    Downgrade takes effect at end of billing period
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
When downgrading, ensure your usage is within the new tier's limits. Papers exceeding the limit become read-only.
</Warning>

### Cancel subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings ‚Üí Subscription**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Find the **Cancel Subscription** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose timing">
    Cancel immediately or at period end
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm cancellation">
    Confirm and receive email confirmation
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens when you cancel:**
- Access continues until period end
- Automatically downgraded to Free tier
- Data retained for 30 days
- Can resubscribe anytime

<Note>
No refunds for partial billing periods, but you keep access until your paid period ends.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch tiers anytime?">
    Yes! You can upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing period. Your data transfers seamlessly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I downgrade?">
    All your data is preserved. If you exceed the new limits (e.g., 500 papers ‚Üí 5), existing papers become read-only until you delete some or upgrade again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused papers/messages roll over?">
    No, monthly limits reset on the 1st of each month. However, boost credits never expire.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a student discount?">
    Student discounts are coming soon! Email support@freiya.app with your .edu email to join the waitlist.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    We offer refunds within 14 days of initial purchase if Freiya isn't the right fit. Contact support@freiya.app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe. International payments are supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
    Yes! We use Stripe for payment processing. We never store your payment information on our servers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    Subscription pausing is not currently available, but you can cancel and resubscribe anytime without losing data (retained for 30 days).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
